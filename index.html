FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore the questions below to learn more about the innovative solutions that Astra Healthcare provides and how they can enhance your healthcare experience.

Where are Astra Healthcare's products made?

Our products are manufactured in several state-of-the-art facilities across India, all of which operate under strict guidelines to ensure quality and safety.

Where can I find information about your products?

Detailed information about our products, including indications, usage, and scientific research, can be found on the “Products” section of our website.

Do I need a prescription to buy your products?

Yes, most of our pharmaceutical products require a prescription from a licensed healthcare provider. Over-the-counter product availability varies by region.

How do you ensure the safety of your medicines?

All our medicines undergo rigorous testing for safety and efficacy in various stages of clinical trials before they are approved by regulatory authorities in India.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support can be reached via email, phone. Contact details are available under the “Contact Us” section.

Are your products available worldwide?

While we strive to make our products as accessible as possible, availability can vary depending on the country and its specific health regulations. Currently we operate only in India.
